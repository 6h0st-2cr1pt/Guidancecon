About Guidance Connect

Guidance Connect is a Digital Counseling Appointment System designed to provide college students with a simple, confidential, and accessible way to book and manage counseling appointments online. It supports mental health and well-being by connecting students with qualified guidance counselors assigned to their programs, ensuring privacy and ease of use within a secure digital environment. The system promotes timely access to guidance on academic, personal, and career-related issues.

How the process works:

  • REGISTRATION AND ACCESS: Students start by registering on the website, agreeing to a privacy notice and terms of services to ensure data confidentiality. Once logged in, they access a personalized student portal.
  • DASHBOARD OVERVIEW : After logging in, students are presented with a dashboard that provides them quick access to the main features of the system.
  • BOOK APPOINTMENT : This area allows students to schedule counseling sessions. By clicking "book now," they can select the date, time, and their preferred guidance counselor.
  • NOTIFICATION: This section displays important announcements, allowing students to view appointment updates, reminders, and other important messages.
  • MY APPOINTMENT: This section allows students to view and manage their counseling appointment schedule. It displays upcoming, completed, or cancelled appointments that help students to stay updated on their session status.

Additional Information

The system operates Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM.

The system adheres to ethical standards, ensuring all data is used solely for counseling purposes and protected under privacy agreements.
